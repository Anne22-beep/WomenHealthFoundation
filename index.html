Our Story

Healthy Women Foundation, Non-Profit Organization

Healthy Woman Foundation was founded by the Sapphire Ladies (Group 4). We believe the world will only become a better place once women are educated and empowered about their health Through this, we can achieve good health and wellbeing for all.

Our Mission

Attain good health and wellbeing for all by empowering women.
